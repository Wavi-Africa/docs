---
title: Your first email
description: Send a transactional email via the Wavi API.
sidebar:
  order: 3
---

Add your sending domain under **Domains**, paste the DKIM and SPF records into your DNS, wait for verification, then:

```bash
curl https://api.waviafrica.com/v1/email \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer wavi_live_..." \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "to": "you@company.com",
    "from": "hello@yourdomain.com",
    "subject": "Welcome",
    "html": "<p>Hi there,</p>"
  }'
```
